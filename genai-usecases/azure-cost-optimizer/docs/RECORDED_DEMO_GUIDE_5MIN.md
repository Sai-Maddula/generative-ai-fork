## üéôÔ∏è VOICE-OVER RECORDING TIPS

### **Technical Setup:**
- **Microphone:** Condenser mic (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB)
- **Environment:** Quiet room with sound dampening
- **Recording Software:** Audacity, Adobe Audition, or Descript
- **Format:** WAV 48kHz 24-bit (lossless quality)

### **Delivery Style:**
- **Pace:** Slightly slower than conversational (120-130 WPM)
- **Tone:** Confident, professional, energetic (not monotone)
- **Emphasis:** Punch keywords: "30 seconds," "HITL," "explainable," "zero outages"
- **Pauses:** Let visuals breathe (pause after key statements)
- **Energy:** Start strong (Scene 1), peak at Scene 3 (HITL), confident close

### **Recording Strategy:**
- Record each scene separately (easier editing)
- Do 3 takes per scene (pick best or composite)
- Record room tone for 10 seconds (helps with noise reduction)
- Listen back immediately (catch pacing issues early)
